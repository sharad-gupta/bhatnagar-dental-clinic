Comprehensive Orthodontic Treatment for all patients

Dr. Manish Bhatnagar is a dental professional with specialized education in orthodontic procedures. With Orthodontics, he can help people to straighten misaligned teeth. Most people assume that orthodontic treatment is for kids. However, specialized orthodontic treatments for adults are also available.
There are some specific conditions that do require orthodontic treatment and those conditions include

Misplaced Midline
Occurs when the center of your upper front teeth does not line up with the center of your lower teeth Overbite: This occurs when the upper teeth sit too far in front of the bottom teeth and you end up with a buck teeth appearance

Spacing
Gaps and spaces occur in the mouth sometimes and these spaces and gaps need to be closed to allow you to have a straight smile

Underbite
This is when the lower teeth stick out farther in front of the upper set of teeth. This type of look is often referred to as a bulldog appearance

Crowding
This occurs when your teeth are too close together and the dental ridge is unable to accommodate them and the teeth appear crooked or spaced oddly such as one in front of another

Open Bite
This is when the biting surfaces present a gap in between them when you bite down and apply pressure

Crossbite
This is when the upper teeth do not come down over the front lower teeth when you bite down
